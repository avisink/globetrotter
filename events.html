Unique Events in Austin

South by Southwest (SXSW)

Address: Various locations throughout Austin, TX

South by Southwest (SXSW) is an annual conglomerate of parallel film, interactive media, and music festivals and conferences. It attracts creatives and professionals from around the world, offering a platform for networking, showcasing new products, and enjoying a wide array of performances and screenings.

Visit here for more information about SXSW.

Austin City Limits (ACL) Music Festival

Address: Zilker Park, 2100 Barton Springs Rd, Austin, TX 78704

The Austin City Limits (ACL) Music Festival is a two-weekend event featuring a diverse lineup of artists from various genres. Held in Zilker Park, the festival includes multiple stages, food vendors, and activities, making it a must-attend event for music lovers.

Visit here for more information about ACL.

Eeyore's Birthday Party

Address: Pease District Park, 1100 Kingsbury St, Austin, TX 78703

Eeyore's Birthday Party is a whimsical, family-friendly event celebrating the beloved character from A.A. Milne's "Winnie the Pooh" series. The event features live music, costume contests, food, and games, all in a laid-back, community-focused atmosphere.

Visit here for more information about Eeyore's Birthday Party.

Austin Trail of Lights

Address: Zilker Park, 2100 Barton Springs Rd, Austin, TX 78704

The Austin Trail of Lights is a festive holiday event featuring over 2 million lights, 40 displays, and numerous holiday activities. Held annually in Zilker Park, it offers a magical experience for visitors of all ages, complete with food vendors, live entertainment, and photo opportunities.

Visit here for more information about the Austin Trail of Lights.
